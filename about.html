Established in the heart of Bettendorf, Iowa, we have been a trusted name in the beverage industry since 2001. Our story began with a passion for providing nutritious and delicious juice options to families, schools, and businesses right here in our hometown. We understand the values and preferences of our community, which is why we're dedicated to delivering the finest quality juice products that meet the highest standards of taste and nutrition. However, our commitment reaches far beyond our local borders. We are part of the larger Penny Juice family, a brand recognized nationwide for its dedication to health-conscious choices and excellence in beverage solutions.

OUR MISSION

At Penny Juice we dedicate our full efforts to make childcare easier and accessible. We also believe that great taste and good health should always go hand in hand. That's why we are dedicated to sourcing the freshest fruits and using state-of-the-art technology to create our 100% blended fruit juice concentrates. Our products meet the stringent requirements of the USDA and FDA, ensuring that they are not only delicious but also nutritionally sound.
