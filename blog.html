Making our juices are quiet simple! Here are the general steps:

Ingredients:

Penny Juice concentrate

Water (filtered or tap water)

Equipment:

Measuring cup

Pitcher or container

Mixing spoon

Instructions:

1. Measure the concentrate: Check the instructions on the Penny Juice concentrate container to determine the recommended ratio of concentrate to water. Typically, itâ€™s one part concentrate and seven parts water.

2. Pour Concentrate: Pour the desired amount of Penny Juice concentrate into a measuring cup. With the measured concentrate, pour it into a pitcher or container.

3. Add Water: In a pitcher or container, add the appropriate amount of water based on the ratio specified on the concentrate container. Slowly pour the water into the pitcher.

4. Mix Thoroughly: Use a mixing spoon to stir the concentrate and water together. Ensure that they are well combined. You may need to stir for a minute or two to fully dissolve the concentrate.

5. Serve: Pour the freshly prepared Penny Juice into glasses or cups. You can add ice if you prefer a chilled drink.

If you have any leftover prepared juice, you can store it in the refrigerator for a short period. However, it is not necesarry to refrigerate the concentrate before or after opening it.
